Our Story

From the Fields of Westmeath to Your Table

At Conor's Grill we work directly with Irish farmers across Co. Westmeath and the surrounding midlands. Every cut is hand-selected, dry-aged for a minimum of 28 days, and seared over an open flame the way it was meant to be.

We don't do frozen. We don't do shortcuts. We do one thing, and we do it better than anyone else in Mullingar.
