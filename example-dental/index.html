Our story

Dentistry that puts the patient first.

We opened Example Dental in 2011 in a converted Georgian building on Westport's Quay. Our name comes from the nearby coast — a nod to the town we call home and the rhythms of care we try to provide.

A practice built on honesty, not upselling.

When Dr. Aoife Brennan returned to Westport after training at NUI Galway and a stint in Melbourne, she had one thing in mind: a dental practice that felt nothing like the ones she'd grown up dreading. No garish posters, no awkward silences, no surprise bills.

The early years were small — just Aoife and one nurse in two rooms. Word spread slowly, then quickly. By 2015 we'd expanded into the full building. By 2018, we'd added a specialist orthodontist. Today we're a team of four clinicians and six support staff, still in the same building, still with the same approach.

We won't recommend a treatment you don't need. We'll explain everything before we do it. And if we think another practice is better placed to help you, we'll say so.

AB
Dr. Aoife Brennan
Principal Dentist & Founder

Aoife grew up in Westport and returned here after graduating from NUI Galway in 2008 and spending two years working in Melbourne. She founded Example Dental in 2011 with the ambition of building a practice the town could genuinely rely on. Her approach is gentle and methodical — she's particularly experienced with anxious patients and has a special interest in minimally invasive techniques.

Dr. Ciarán Walsh
Cosmetic & Restorative Dentist

Ciarán trained at Trinity College Dublin and joined Example Dental in 2014 after several years in a busy Dublin city-centre practice. His focus is on restorative and cosmetic work — veneers, crowns, bonding, and implants — and he's known for his eye for natural-looking results. He completed advanced digital smile design training in 2022.

Dr. Sinéad Ó'Connor
Specialist Orthodontist

Sinéad is our resident orthodontist, having completed her MSc at King's College London after her undergraduate degree at UCD. She joined Example Dental in 2018 and has since treated hundreds of adults and teenagers. She's a certified Invisalign provider and takes particular satisfaction in cases that make the most difference.

Niamh Doyle

Niamh has been with Example Dental since 2013 and is, by her own admission, slightly obsessed with gum health.
