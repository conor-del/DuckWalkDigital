Our Story

Rooted in the Wexford countryside

Example Construction is a naturalistic countryside homes maker based in Wexford, in operation since 2007. We work closely with the landscape rather than against it, shaping homes from timber, stone and light that feel settled the moment they're finished.

Every build starts with the site itself — the treeline, the slope, the way the light moves through the day — and the house follows from there.
