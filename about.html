About

The studio behind Waddles

We're Duck Walk Digital — a small studio that designs and builds websites for businesses who'd rather stand out than blend in.

Duck Walk Digital was founded by Conor Burns. Conor has been fascinated with all things business and marketing since he was 12 years old — he was obsessed with learning about how the largest corporations globally came to be and what strategies they used to dominate competitors, and fixated with learning everything he could online. At 13, Conor started learning web development, first on WordPress, then began learning how to code, from simple HTML elements to complex JavaScript scripts. At 14 he created his first Shopify store and got obsessed with ecommerce and the Shopify platform.

Duck Walk Digital is committed to creating not only appealing, but highly functional and profitable websites. Sales, appointments and enquiries are at the core of all of our websites — we strategically place the strongest images and craft the most suitable copy to do whatever it needs to, from explaining your services in a no-nonsense, concise way to eloquently describing your most premium products, all in an effort to make your digital shopfront a distinguished masterpiece.

We believe exceptional work is born from an obsession with the finer details. Every website we create is passionately crafted to balance aesthetics with performance, allowing you to build trust with prospects, strengthen your online presence, and help your business excel. We don't consider a project finished until every interaction feels intuitive and every page performs exactly as it should — refining every project until it truly reflects the business behind it. Whether it's a new local business, a growing ecommerce store, or an established industry leader, we go the same lengths for everyone.

Websites with bite, backed by Waddles. Built to turn heads and designed to win customers.
